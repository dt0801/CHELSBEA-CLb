Stamford Bridge

On April 28, 1877, Stamford Bridge with 5,000 seats officially opened for the first time. It was a grand event organized by its owner, one of the wealthiest Lords in London, and it was the product of architect Archibald Leitch - a man no one in the design world knew about. . After 28 years, the second largest stadium in England (only inferior to Crystal Palace's yard) is still standing there, but its owner is not, even living half-dead in poverty. Thomas Tuchel

29-08-1973

Tuchel led Mainz to a stable match in the national championship for 5 seasons at this club. The shaping of the game and the energetic, attacking football at Mainz has earned Tuchel much praise. He also built his reputation when he focused on nurturing and developing the talents of young players.[4] He left Mainz in 2014 due to a financial dispute; A year later, Tuchel was appointed head coach by another Bundesliga club, Borussia Dortmund. Tuchel won the DFB-Pokal with Dortmund, before he was sacked in 2017.
